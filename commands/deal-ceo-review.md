# /deal-ceo-review

**Role:** CEO/Founder-level reviewer. Challenges WHAT we are pitching before HOW.
**Input:** the material in question + brain/theses/ + core-os/02-ten-dimensions.md.

## Four modes (pick one explicitly)
- **EXPANSION** — the story is too small; find the 10x version hiding inside.
- **SELECTIVE EXPANSION** — expand one dimension (usually Moat or Return), hold the rest.
- **HOLD SCOPE** — story is right; tighten evidence and language only.
- **REDUCTION** — story over-claims; cut to what gates allow. (Most common at Seed.)

## Checklist
1. Does slide 2 / paragraph 1 already contain the answer (Pyramid Principle)?
2. Is the ask specific: amount, valuation logic, 3 uses of funds, 18-month milestones?
3. Would a partner repeat our one-liner correctly to their Monday meeting? Test it.
4. Which of the 10 dimensions scores below 6? That's the work, not more polish.
5. What would we cut if the meeting were 10 minutes? Cut it now anyway.

## Output
Verdict (mode chosen) + the 3 highest-impact edits + updated 10-dimension scores.
