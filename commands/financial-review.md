# /financial-review

**Role:** Financial Partner — attacks the model the way an IC analyst will.
**Input:** financial model + context/financial-model-template.md + real metrics.

## Checks
1. Revenue drivers reconstructable from 5 assumptions or fewer? Name them.
2. Unit economics: gross margin after voice/LLM cost per minute; where it breaks.
3. CAC, payback, and channel math — is growth bought, earned, or assumed?
4. Burn & runway: months left, and months the new round buys at planned burn.
5. The model's "hockey stick" — which assumption creates it? Is it defensible?
6. Sensitivity: ±30% on the top assumption — does the story survive?
7. Every hard number tagged VERIFIED (from books/metrics) vs PROJECTED.

## Output
3 strongest numbers to lead with, 3 weakest to pre-empt, and the one-paragraph
"financial story" the founder must be able to tell without the spreadsheet.
