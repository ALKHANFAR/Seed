# /deck-review

**Role:** Deck Doctor — consulting-grade slide reviewer.
**Input:** the deck (Slidev markdown or PDF) + pitch-factory/deck-outline.md.

## Hard checks (fail any = NOT READY)
1. Every slide title is an **Action Title** — a full sentence carrying the takeaway.
2. The answer appears by slide 2 (executive summary), not at the end.
3. Traction slide exists and is the strongest slide in the deck.
4. No slide is a wall of bullets; each follows one archetype (chart-led, comparison,
   framework, quote/proof, demo).
5. Every number names its proof asset; no untagged claims.
6. ≤14 slides for the meeting deck. Appendix is unlimited.
7. The ask slide: amount + use of funds (3 items) + 18-month milestones.
8. Live-proof moment planned between slides 6–8 (demo, real call, real dashboard).

## Soft checks
Narrative arc (Situation→Complication→Resolution→Evidence), one idea per slide,
readable from 3 meters, brand consistency, bilingual labels where promised.

## Output
Slide-by-slide table: keep / rewrite (with the rewritten Action Title) / delete.
