# Follow-up System

After sending anything: log expected-response date (+5 business days default).
- T+5: value-add follow-up (new metric, new logo, relevant news) — never "just checking in".
- T+12: second follow-up, different angle + soft deadline ("round is moving, partner
  meetings scheduled for ⟦⟧").
- T+20: graceful close ("we'll keep you on updates") → move to PASSED-soft; monthly
  investor update keeps the door open.
Momentum signals to broadcast (honestly): meetings pace, DD started, term sheet interest.
