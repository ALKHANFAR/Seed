# Fund Playbook Method

A playbook is ONLY as good as its verified intel. Never pitch from assumptions
about a fund. Before any first meeting, complete the fund's brain/funds/ page:

1. Research (their site, recent announcements, portfolio, partner interviews):
   stage, check size, thesis keywords, relevant portfolio, decision process.
   Everything found gets a date + source. Everything else stays ⟦TO VERIFY⟧.
2. Conflict check: portfolio companies that compete with us? Synergize?
3. Angle: which 2–3 of our 10 dimensions does THIS fund weight most?
4. Warm path: who can introduce us? (cold is the last resort)
5. Tailoring: opening line + which proof asset to lead with.

The three playbooks here are skeletons; their intel sections must be filled by
research, not by an LLM's memory (funds change fast — stale intel kills credibility).
